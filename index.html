Set Up Your Canon Printer with canon.com/ijsetup


Visit canon.com/ijsetup, enter your Canon printer model, and download the appropriate driver or setup software. Install the software, connect your printer to Wi-Fi, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup and start printing.

Step-by-Step Guide to Canon Printer Setup

  • 1. Open your browser and go to canon.com/ijsetup.
  • 2. Enter your Canon printer model and click "Search".
  • 3. Click on "Download" to get the appropriate driver or setup software for your device.
  • 4. Once the file is downloaded, open it and follow the installation instructions.
  • 5. Turn on your Canon printer and ensure it's connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer or mobile device.
  • 6. Launch the Canon setup utility and follow the on-screen steps to connect your printer.
  • 7. After setup is complete, you can begin printing, scanning, and managing your printer with ease.
